Trust, Compliance & Risk Disclosure

Whytegod operates as a peer-to-peer (P2P) crypto trading desk. We prioritize transparency, security, and responsible execution for all buy and sell requests.

1. Nature of Our Service

Whytegod is not a bank, not a brokerage, and not an automated exchange. All transactions are handled manually and reviewed by our operations desk.

By submitting an order, you acknowledge that you are requesting a peer-to-peer transaction facilitated by Whytegod.

2. Risk Disclosure

Cryptocurrency trading involves significant risk, including price volatility, liquidity risk, and regulatory uncertainty.

  • Crypto prices may change rapidly
  • Transactions are irreversible once confirmed
  • You are responsible for providing accurate details

You should only trade amounts you can afford to lose.

3. Compliance & Verification

To prevent fraud, money laundering, and abuse, Whytegod may request:

  • Proof of payment
  • Wallet ownership confirmation
  • Identity verification (where required)

Failure to comply may result in order cancellation.

4. No Financial Advice

Whytegod does not provide investment, legal, or financial advice. All decisions are made solely by the user.

5. Limitation of Liability

Whytegod shall not be held liable for losses resulting from:

  • Market volatility
  • User error
  • Third-party service failures
  • Regulatory changes

6. Contact & Resolution

For disputes, clarification, or support, communication will be conducted via the email used during order submission.

We aim to resolve all issues fairly and professionally.
