WHYTEGOD

A Public Crypto Reference Protocol

What This Is

Whytegod is a read-only protocol that explains what crypto systems actually do — before you trust them, before you use them, before you lose money.

No accounts. No wallets. No execution. Only rules, math, and consequences.

Who This Is For

  • Ordinary people who don’t want to guess
  • Developers who want neutral verification
  • Users tired of hidden risks
  • Anyone who values clarity over persuasion

What Whytegod Does (v1)

1. Action Translator

Explains what a crypto action will do, what must happen, what might happen, and what cannot happen — in plain language.

2. Claim Verifier

Separates mathematical truth from assumptions, human control, and undefined promises.

3. Protocol Risk Lens

Shows control concentration, upgrade authority, immutability, and dependency risks — without opinions.

What Whytegod Does Not Do

  • No custody of funds
  • No transactions
  • No investment advice
  • No promotions
  • No payments

Whytegod cannot benefit from your decisions.

Why This Exists

Crypto became complex faster than humans could understand it. Interfaces became simpler — rules became hidden.

Whytegod exposes rules again.

Governance

Whytegod has no owner. Truth cannot be voted on. Math cannot be marketed.

Status

  • Phase 0 — Declaration
  • Phase 1 — Reference Engine
  • Phase 2 — Public Verification Tools
  • Phase 3 — Optional Utility Layer (only if required)
