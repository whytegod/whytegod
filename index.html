What is Whytegod?

Whytegod is not an exchange, not a wallet, and not a trading platform. It is a protocol designed to help humans express intent clearly and let systems execute it safely.

Why it exists

Most systems force people to think like machines. Whytegod flips that model — the system adapts to human intent.

What makes it different

• No custody of user funds
• No hidden operators
• No forced speculation
• No permission required

Whytegod is an idea expressed as software. No promises. No shortcuts. Just rules.
