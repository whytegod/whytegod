What Whytegod Is

Whytegod is a neutral protocol that explains the structure and risk of crypto actions in plain language.

It does not predict outcomes. It does not recommend actions. It does not promise profit.

Whytegod translates crypto behavior into cause and effect so ordinary people can understand what they are consenting to.

What Whytegod Is Not

  • An exchange
  • A wallet
  • Financial advice
  • A trading platform
  • An investment product

Whytegod does not take custody of assets and does not execute transactions.

How Whytegod Is Used

Describe a crypto action in one sentence. Whytegod explains what that action depends on, who controls it, and what cannot be guaranteed.

If something cannot be explained clearly, it cannot be trusted.
