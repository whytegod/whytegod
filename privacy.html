Privacy Policy

Whytegod respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only when required by law or to prevent fraud.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational, legal, and compliance purposes.

6. User Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us via the email used during order submission.
