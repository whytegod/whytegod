Privacy

Whytegod does not observe users.

No Data Collection

Whytegod does not collect personal data, behavioral data, or usage metrics.

There are no accounts, identifiers, or profiles.

No Tracking

No cookies are used.

No analytics software is embedded.

No fingerprinting techniques are applied.

No Logging

The protocol does not log IP addresses, timestamps, or interaction metadata.

What is not recorded cannot be analyzed.

Minimal Exposure

Interaction with the protocol requires no registration, no identity, and no disclosure.

Users reveal only what they choose externally.

Third-Party Independence

Whytegod does not embed third-party services.

Any external platforms accessed by users operate under their own privacy terms.

Persistence

There is no user state to persist.

There is nothing to delete because nothing is stored.

Principle

Privacy is not a feature.

It is the absence of unnecessary observation.

Conclusion

Whytegod cannot leak data.

It does not have any.
